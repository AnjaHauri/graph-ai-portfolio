About the Course

The Generative AI for Work & Research course is an 8-week live cohort program offered by GRAPH Courses, University of Geneva. It focuses on no-code AI tools for task automation, data analysis, and research workflows.

Key Modules

  • Websites, Documents & Presentations – using LLMs for papers, grants, and presentations
  • AI-Automated Data Analysis – quick analysis, dashboards, and automated reports
  • GenAI for Research & Text Analysis – literature reviews, classification, summarization
  • Custom GPTs and Chatbots – building specialized AI assistants
  • AI for Task Automation – workflows, monitoring, browser automation
  • GenAI for Communication & Outreach – figures, videos, visualization
  • Capstone Project & Showcase

Features include: weekly workshops, live support, peer collaboration, and a capstone project with real-world AI applications.
